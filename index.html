Action Girls Africa (AGA) is a women-led national Non-Governmental Organization, non-profit charitable Organization that aims at providing humanitarian assistance to the needy.

AGA started and established as an association of Girls and has now advanced to an organization officially registered in January, 2017 as a National Non-Governmental Organization with the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission (RRC) of The Republic of South Sudan in conformity within the meaning of Chapter 3, Section: 10 of NGO Act 2016 with Reg. No. 585 for its legal operations. Learn more

Our mission

AGA exists to promote creative ideas that inspire and transform lives of the vulnerable and disadvantaged groups who are the girls, women and children through engaging them in empowerment and sustainable developmental programs.

Our vision

A lead empowerment and transforming organization for a society where Girls and Women are Self-reliant, Conversant and Sentient of Striving for their Rights.
